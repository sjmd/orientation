Readings, Watchings, & Listenings (Oh my!)

Topics will often explore an idea with steps that include the following:

  • Readings (a chapter from a book, an article, a blog post, an essay, etc.)
  • Watchings (a YouTube video, TED talk, documentary clip, etc.)
  • Listenings (a podcast episode, interview clip, etc.)

Generally, we’ll try to give you a few options of different ways to explore the same idea (e.g., a video or article that both cover the same subject).

Everything is optional, and everything is recommended. It’s up to you to pick and choose what you take in.
